Snakehead (Channa striata)

  • Scientific Name: Channa striata
  • Common Name: Ikan Gabus
  • Habitat: Found in various freshwater environments like rivers, swamps, and rice fields throughout Indonesia.
  • Fun Fact: They are famous for their ability to migrate over land! By wriggling their bodies, they can move between pools of water, especially during the rainy season.
  • Conservation Status: Least Concern

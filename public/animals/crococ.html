Crocodile

  • Scientific Name: Crocodylus porosus
  • Habitat: Rivers, marshes, and billabongs across Southeast Asia and Northern Australia.
  • Fun Fact: Crocodiles can go for months without a big meal! Because they are cold-blooded and have a very slow metabolism, they can survive a long time just by resting.
  • Conservation Status: Least Concern

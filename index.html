Faaiz & Aulia: A Wildlife Photography Journey

Our recent trip to Ragunan Zoo was more than just a weekend getaway; it was a thrilling journey into the heart of the wild. Armed with our cameras, Faaiz and Aulia spent the day exploring the lush, green pathways, capturing the raw beauty of the animals through our lenses.

From the majestic stare of the Sumatran tiger to the playful antics of the primates at the Schmutzer Primate Center, every click of the shutter told a unique story of nature. It was a perfect blend of fun, laughter, and a shared passion for wildlife photography, leaving us with a gallery full of memories and a deeper appreciation for the incredible creatures we share this planet with.
